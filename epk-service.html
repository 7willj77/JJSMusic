Professional Electronic Press Kit Service for Musicians and Bands

A strong Electronic Press Kit helps you present your music professionally when contacting labels, radio stations, playlist curators, blogs, promoters and media outlets.

Professional EPK creation for independent artists

JJS Music creates polished, industry-facing EPKs that bring together your story, sound, visuals and key release information in one clear package. The aim is to help you look credible, prepared and easy to work with.

What your EPK can include

  • Professional artist biography
  • Polished press release for your release or campaign
  • High-quality image and branding guidance
  • Direct links to your music, videos and social platforms
  • Clear release information and artist positioning
  • Contact details for press, radio, playlist and industry enquiries

Who this service is for

This service is ideal for independent artists, bands, producers and songwriters preparing to release music, pitch to press, approach playlist curators, contact radio stations or build a more professional artist presence.

Why an EPK matters

Industry contacts are busy. If they have to search for your bio, images, links, release details and contact information, you make their job harder. A clear EPK gives them what they need quickly and helps you make a stronger first impression.

A well-built EPK does not just describe your music. It presents your artist brand, explains your story, supports your release and gives people a reason to take you seriously.

Make your release easier to pitch

Whether you are sending your music to blogs, radio, labels, playlist curators or local press, a professional EPK helps you communicate with confidence. JJS Music structures your materials so your pitch feels focused, credible and ready to send.
